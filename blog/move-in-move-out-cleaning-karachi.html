The Ultimate Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning Checklist for Karachi

Moving house in Karachi is stressful. Between finding a mover, packing boxes, and navigating the traffic, the last thing you want to worry about is scrubbing floors.

However, cleaning is a critical part of the process. Whether you are leaving a rental and want your security deposit back, or moving into a new home that needs to be sanitized before your furniture arrives, you need a plan.

Here is the ultimate Move-In/Move-Out cleaning service Karachi checklist.

1 The "Move-Out" Clean: Leaving on Good Terms

Landlords in Karachi are strict about deposits. To ensure you get your money back, you need to leave the property in top condition with proper deep cleaning services Karachi.

  • Remove all Nails and Screws: Fill the holes with spackle.
  • Deep Clean Appliances: If the stove or fridge stays, they must be grease-free.
  • The "Hidden" Dust: Clean the tops of cupboards, ceiling fans, and inside drawers.
  • Bathrooms: Descale taps and scrub grout. This is the #1 area landlords check.
  • Floors: A final sweep and mop after all boxes are gone.
2 The "Move-In" Clean: A Fresh Start

You don't know who lived there before you or what their hygiene standards were. A deep cleaning service is essential before you unpack.

  • Sanitize High-Touch Areas: Door handles, light switches, and cabinet knobs need disinfecting.
  • Kitchen Degreasing: Even if it looks clean, give the kitchen a deep scrub to remove old cooking smells and grease.
  • Toilet Replacement/Sanitization: We recommend deep sanitizing the toilet or replacing the seat entirely.
  • Pest Control: An empty house is the perfect time to spray for pests before bringing in your family.
