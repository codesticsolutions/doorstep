The 5-Step Checklist for Safely Hiring Domestic Staff in Karachi (Maids, Cooks, & Nannies)

Hiring domestic staff in Karachi—whether it's a Karachi maid, a cook for your home, or a nanny for your child—is a decision that requires the utmost trust. You are inviting someone into your private space, around your family and your valuables.

Unfortunately, finding reliable staff is difficult. The informal market is filled with risks, from unreliability to theft.

How can you hire safely? Here is a 5-step checklist.

1

Define Your Needs (Specifically)

Don't just say "I need a maid." Be specific about your requirements to find the right match.

Tasks

Is it just cleaning? Or also laundry, ironing, and dishes?

Hours

Do you need a live-in, a full-time (8-hour) person, or a part-time (2-3 hour) cleaner?

Special Requirements

For specific roles like cooks or nannies, define cuisine preferences, number of meals, child ages, etc.

A clear job description is the first step toward finding the right person.

For a Cook

What cuisine? How many meals per day? Who does the grocery shopping?

For a Nanny

What age are your children? Do you need help with homework or just supervision?

For a Maid

Specific cleaning tasks? Laundry? Cooking? Pet care?

2

The Vetting Problem (The Hardest Part)

This is where most people fail. True vetting is more than just asking for a CNIC.

Warning: This process is time-consuming and difficult, which is why most people skip it—at their own peril.

Background Check

Do they have a police clearance? Verify their criminal history.

Reference Check

You must call their previous employers. Ask them: "Were they reliable?", "Were they honest?", and "Would you hire them again?"

Address Verification

Is their provided address real? Verify their residential information.

3

The Interview

Once you have a vetted candidate, the interview is crucial to assess compatibility and professionalism.

Situational Questions

Ask: "What would you do if my child fell and got hurt?" or "What would you do if you accidentally broke a plate?"

Observe Personal Hygiene

Note their appearance, cleanliness, and punctuality.

Trust Your Instincts

If you feel uncomfortable for any reason, do not proceed. Your intuition is a valuable safety measure.

4

The Paid Trial

Never hire someone on the spot. Always agree to a 1-2 week paid trial period.

Observe Skills & Work Ethic

See how they perform actual tasks and handle responsibilities.

Assess Family Compatibility

Determine if their personality and work style fit well with your family dynamics.

Confirm Reliability

Verify their punctuality and consistency over several days.
