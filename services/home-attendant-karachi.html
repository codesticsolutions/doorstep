Frequently Asked Questions About Home Health Care

Q: What's the difference between a nurse and a home health attendant?

A: Nurses provide medical treatments and clinical care, while health attendants assist with daily living activities and basic health monitoring under nurse supervision.

Q: Are your female home nurses available 24/7?

A: Yes, we provide 24/7 female home nurse services for patients requiring continuous care and monitoring.

Q: What medical conditions do your attendants handle?

A: Our attendants are trained for elderly care, post-stroke recovery, diabetes management, post-surgery care, and chronic illness support.

Q: How quickly can I hire a home health attendant?

A: We can deploy certified attendants within 24 hours after medical assessment and documentation.
