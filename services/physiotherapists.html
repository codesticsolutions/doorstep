Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services We Offer

We understand that every patient has unique needs. That's why we offer a wide range of specialized physiotherapy services:

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

Treatment for back pain, neck pain, joint pain, arthritis, and soft tissue injuries. Manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and modalities for pain relief.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation for stroke, spinal cord injuries, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and balance disorders. Focus on functional recovery and neuroplasticity.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Specialized care for elderly patients to improve mobility, strength, and balance, reduce fall risk, and manage age-related conditions like osteoporosis and arthritis.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Get back to your game safely with targeted rehabilitation for sports-related injuries including ligament tears, muscle strains, and overuse injuries.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Accelerate recovery after joint replacement (knee/hip), spinal surgery, fracture repair, and other orthopedic procedures. Regain strength and range of motion.

Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy

Breathing exercises and endurance training for patients recovering from heart surgery, COPD, or other respiratory conditions.
