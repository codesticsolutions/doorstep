Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Domestic Staff Questions

Here are some of the most common questions clients ask us about hiring and managing domestic staff. We’ve provided clear answers to help you make the right decision with confidence.

What types of domestic staff do you provide?

We provide a wide range of staff including housemaids, babysitters, drivers, cooks, nurses, attendants, gatekeepers, gardeners, and more — tailored to meet your household needs.

How do you ensure staff are trustworthy and reliable?

All our staff members go through a strict background check, verification process, and professional training to ensure they are reliable, skilled, and trustworthy before placement.

Can I hire staff on a part-time or full-time basis?

Yes, you can hire domestic staff based on your requirement — whether part-time, full-time, or live-in. We offer flexible options to suit your household’s needs.

What if I face any issue after hiring staff?

We provide complete customer support. If you face any issue with the hired staff, we assist you immediately and arrange a suitable replacement if required.
