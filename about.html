Answers to Common Domestic Staff Questions

Here are some of the most common questions clients ask us about hiring and managing domestic staff – maids, cooks, nannies, drivers, nurses, and caregivers in Karachi. We’ve provided clear answers to help you make the right decision with confidence.

What types of domestic staff do you provide in Karachi?

We provide a wide range of staff including housemaids (full‑time, part‑time, live‑in), cooks and chefs (Desi, Chinese, Continental), nannies and babysitters, drivers, nurses, patient attendants, caregivers, gatekeepers, gardeners, housekeepers, and office boys – all thoroughly screened and verified.

How do you ensure your maids, nannies, and drivers are trustworthy?

All our staff members undergo a strict background check, ID verification, reference checks, and professional skill assessment. We verify every detail to ensure they are reliable, skilled, and trustworthy before placement. We also offer a 3‑month free replacement guarantee for your peace of mind.

Can I hire a cook, driver, or maid on a part‑time or full‑time basis?

Yes, absolutely. You can hire domestic staff based on your requirement – whether part‑time, full‑time, live‑in, or live‑out. We offer flexible options to suit your household’s needs, including temporary or permanent placements.

Do you provide nurses and patient attendants for home care in Karachi?

Yes, we provide qualified nurses, caregivers, and patient attendants for elderly care, post‑surgery support, chronic illness management, injection administration, and 24‑hour home nursing. Our staff is trained to handle medical needs with compassion and professionalism.

What areas of Karachi do you serve?

We serve all areas of Karachi including DHA Phase 1‑8, Clifton, Defence, Gulshan‑e‑Iqbal, Gulistan‑e‑Jauhar, Saddar, North Nazimabad, Korangi, Landhi, Malir, Federal B Area, and more. If you’re in Karachi, we can help!

What if I am not satisfied with the hired staff?

We provide complete customer support. If you face any issue with the hired staff, we assist you immediately and arrange a suitable replacement at no extra cost within the guarantee period. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you provide specialized staff like physiotherapists or gardeners?

Yes, we also provide expert physiotherapists for home rehabilitation, professional gardeners (mali) for lawn care, and experienced housekeepers to manage your household. Whatever your domestic staffing need, we have the right professional.
