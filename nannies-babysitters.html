Frequently Asked Questions

How do you screen your nannies and babysitters?

All our caregivers undergo thorough background checks, reference verification, personal interviews, and medical screenings. We verify their previous childcare experience and require basic first aid and CPR certification for all nannies.

What's the difference between a nanny and a babysitter?

A nanny typically provides regular, ongoing childcare (full-time or part-time) and may have specialized training. Babysitters offer occasional care for shorter periods. We provide both options to match your specific needs.

Do you provide nannies for newborns and infants?

Yes, we specialize in newborn and infant care services in Karachi. Our nannies trained in newborn care understand feeding schedules, sleep patterns, and developmental milestones for the youngest family members.
